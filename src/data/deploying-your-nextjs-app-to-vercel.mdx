# Deploying Your Next.js App to Vercel

Vercel is the recommended platform for deploying Next.js applications. It offers seamless integration, automatic builds, and global CDN for fast performance. In this article, we'll walk through the steps to deploy your Next.js app to Vercel.

## Why Choose Vercel?

- **Zero Configuration**: Deploy with minimal setup.
- **Automatic SSL**: Free HTTPS for your site.
- **Global CDN**: Fast content delivery worldwide.
- **Preview Deployments**: Test changes before going live.

## Deployment Steps

1. **Push your code to GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket.**
2. **Sign up at [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) and import your repository.**
3. **Follow the prompts to configure your project.**
4. **Click 'Deploy'.**

Vercel will build and deploy your app automatically. You'll get a live URL and preview links for each pull request.

## Custom Domains

Add your own domain in the Vercel dashboard for a professional touch.

## Conclusion

Deploying with Vercel is fast and easy, making it the perfect choice for Next.js projects. Enjoy instant updates, global performance, and a hassle-free workflow.
